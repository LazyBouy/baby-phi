- **Plan said** (plan-archive line 1227, §Part 4 P3 Deliverables): *"4 new listeners — TemplateCFireListener subscribes to ManagesEdgeCreated ... TemplateDFireListener subscribes to HasAgentSupervisorEdgeCreated..."* — resolver trait scoping not explicit.
- **Reality (shipped state at current HEAD)**: Two distinct resolver traits match concept scoping: `TemplateCAdoptionArResolver::resolve(OrgId) -> Option<AuthRequestId>` and `TemplateDAdoptionArResolver::resolve(ProjectId) -> Option<(OrgId, AuthRequestId)>`. Implementations live in [`server/src/platform/projects/resolvers.rs:107+`](../../../../../../modules/crates/server/src/platform/projects/resolvers.rs#L107) as `RepoTemplateCAdoptionArResolver` + `RepoTemplateDAdoptionArResolver`. - **Root cause**: `concept-doc-not-consulted` (plan didn't surface the per-template scoping pattern that the concept doc mandates).
